INDIAN MOVIES ALL TIME
  1. Hindi: Hindi cinema has been the leader in box office performance since talkies began in 1931. In 2022, Hindi cinema represented 33% of box office revenue.
  2. Telugu: Telugu cinema became the largest film industry in India in terms of box office in 2021. In 2022, Telugu cinema represented 20% of box office revenue.
  3. Tamil: Tamil cinema is nicknamed Kollywood, a portmanteau of the names Kodambakkam, a neighborhood in Chennai, and Hollywood. In 2022, Tamil cinema represented 16% of box office revenue.
